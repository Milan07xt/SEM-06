Help Center

Quick answers for common issues in the attendance system.

How do I register my face?

Go to Register, enter your details, and capture a clear face image in good lighting.

Face not detected?

Center your face, keep camera steady, and ensure your room has enough light.

Attendance not marked?

Try again with a slight blink/movement for liveness, then submit one more frame.

Need admin support?

Use the Contact Us page and share your username plus screenshot of the issue.
