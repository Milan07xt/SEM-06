Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and quick solutions for the attendance system.

How do I mark attendance?

Open Mark Attendance, allow camera access, center your face, and submit your live frame.

Why does liveness check fail?

Make a slight natural movement (like blinking), keep lighting clear, and avoid blurry camera frames.

I am registered but not recognized. What should I do?

Re-register with a clearer face image and ensure your face is fully visible during attendance capture.

Where can I get support?

Use the Contact Us page for direct support and include your username for faster help.
